How to play

🏃 Your legion auto-runs forward. Drag left/right (or ◀ ▶ / A-D) to steer the whole crowd.

✳️ Hit gates: ×2 / +30 grow your legion · ÷2 / −15 shrink it. Always take the bigger number.

☣️ Dodge the spinning sawblades — they cull a slice of your crowd (some swing side to side!).

💥 At each stretch's end, CHARGE the barrier — beat its number to SMASH through and roll straight on. Fall short and you're wiped.

♾️ It never ends: the track speeds up every stretch and the scenery shifts. Keep a flawless 🔥 streak for a score multiplier. How far can you get?
