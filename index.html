How to play

🏃 Your legion auto-runs forward. Drag left/right (or ◀ ▶ / A-D) to steer the whole crowd.

✳️ Run through gates: ×2 / +30 grow your legion, −15 shrink it. Always grab the better gate.

☣️ Dodge spinning hazards — they cull a slice of your crowd.

💥 At each stage end, CHARGE the barrier. Crowd ≥ target = smash through & score big. Below target = wiped.

🔥 Clear a stage with zero losses to build a streak score multiplier. 8 stages. Lose if your legion hits 0.
